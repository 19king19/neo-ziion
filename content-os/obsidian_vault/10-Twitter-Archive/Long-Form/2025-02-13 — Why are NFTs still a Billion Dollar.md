# Why are NFTs still a Billion Dollar

Why are NFTs still a Billion Dollar? 

NFT market estimated to have done 8-12 billion in 2024 global worth around 19.1- 20 billion with projected value  to reach up to  $171 Billion by 2032. According to Business Research Insights  (Optimistically). 

While the hype phase died utility use case continues to thrive. Its important to see it as a Blockchain technology and not a fad. 

-Luxury brands using NFTs for verification (e.g., Prada, Louis Vuitton, etc.)
-NFTs in supply chain tracking and authentication
-Real estate transactions involving NFTs
-Ticketing and event access through NFTs
-Government and legal applications (e.g., digital IDs, land registries)
-Gaming and digital asset ownership
-Music and entertainment applications
-airdrops
