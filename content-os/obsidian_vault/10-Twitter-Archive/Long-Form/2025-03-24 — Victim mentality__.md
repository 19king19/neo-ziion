# Victim mentality__

Victim mentality__

is when you see yourself as powerless—when your mindset is shaped by what was done to you, instead of what you’re capable of doing about it. It’s when blame becomes identity, and trauma becomes a ceiling instead of a stepping stone.

But let’s be clear: teaching history is not victim mentality. Teaching history is teaching the blueprint—both the trap and the escape. It’s a strategic act. It’s saying, “Here’s how we were oppressed, so here’s how we never will be again.”

See, the danger isn’t in remembering. The danger is in forgetting.

The danger isn’t in educating children about oppression.

The danger is in them being ignorant of it—because that’s how it repeats.
