# When someone has no context on your life and gives advice theyre not

When someone has no context on your life and gives advice, they're not talking to you, they're talking to themselves.

Most people don't actually want the best version of you, they want the version of you that best serves them.

The best thing I ever did was not listen to other people about what I should do with my life. 

- Alex H.
