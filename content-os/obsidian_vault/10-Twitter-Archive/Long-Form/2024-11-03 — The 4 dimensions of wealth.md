# The 4 dimensions of wealth

The  4 dimensions of wealth:

•Financial Wealth: The money and resources you accumulate.

•Social Wealth: The status, influence, and connections you build.

•Time Wealth: The freedom to control your life and schedule.

•Physical Wealth: The strength, health, and energy you possess.
