# Could SVB cause a Fed Pause

Could SVB cause a “Fed Pause”?  

a Fed pause refers to a temporary halt in changes to monetary policy by the Federal Reserve, often due to a belief that current policy is appropriate for the current economic conditions.

Fed may pause interest rate hikes to avoid slowing down the economy. On the other hand, if the economy is experiencing a downturn or recession, the Fed may pause interest rate cuts to avoid exacerbating inflationary pressures.
