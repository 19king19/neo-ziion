# The boom and bust cycle is a common phenomenon that arises with new

The "boom and bust cycle" is a common phenomenon that arises with new technologies or markets, including NFTs. Here's a more detailed explanation of this concept:

The typical cycle goes like this:

•Innovation Phase - A new technology like NFTs emerges and starts gaining attention as having disruptive potential. Early adopters and investors get excited.

•Boom Phase - Hype and speculation around the innovation causes rapid price rises and a flood of new startups/projects trying to capitalize. Massive growth and "irrational exuberance."

•Bust Phase - The pace of growth proves unsustainable, flaws and limitations become apparent. Prices crash, many projects fold. Disillusionment sets in.

•Consolidation Phase - After the "crypto winter", focus shifts to building real utility and fixing issues. Companies and models with fundamental value persist, hype-driven ones fail.

•Stabilization Phase - The technology finds its core product-market fit and a more stable growth trajectory. Mainstream adoption driven by real-world usefulness rather than speculation.
