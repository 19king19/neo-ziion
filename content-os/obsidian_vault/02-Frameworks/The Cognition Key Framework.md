# The Cognition Key Framework

> The master framework. The book is named after this.

## Definition

The **Cognition Key** is the ability to recognize patterns before they become obvious to the mainstream — and position yourself accordingly.

## The Five Stages

1. **Perception** — See what's actually happening, not what you're told is happening
2. **Pattern** — Identify recurring structures across history, markets, culture
3. **Prediction** — Project forward based on identified patterns
4. **Position** — Move resources and attention before the crowd arrives
5. **Profit** — Capture value from correct positioning

## Key Insight

Most people operate at Stage 1 (Perception) — they react to events. Sovereign thinkers operate at Stage 3-4 — they predict and position before events unfold.
