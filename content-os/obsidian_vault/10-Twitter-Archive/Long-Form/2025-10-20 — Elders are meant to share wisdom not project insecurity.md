# Elders are meant to share wisdom not project insecurity

Elders are meant to share wisdom, not project insecurity. 

Guidance should uplift, not diminish. 

If you’ve never accomplished much in life, check your intentions before calling it mentorship ~ make sure you’re not trying to humble someone for becoming what you never did. 

In Keyism, true elders pour light, not limitation…..
