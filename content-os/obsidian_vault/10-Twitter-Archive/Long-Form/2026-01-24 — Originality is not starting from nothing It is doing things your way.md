# Originality is not starting from nothing It is doing things your way

Originality is not starting from nothing. It is doing things your way. 

The lie people believe is that being inspired cancels originality, when in truth originality is revealed in interpretation, execution, and perspective. 

Chasing trends becomes dangerous when you are only copying, because it trains you to wait for input before you produce output. 

That keeps you reactive, always behind, always dependent on what comes next. 

true creators move from internal DNA, adding their own rhythm, style, and intelligence to whatever they touch.

When you build from who you are, not what is trending, you stop chasing and start leading.
