AI sound scoring  will help democratize high lvl production for content creators

The goal of scoring is to create a seamless and immersive experience that complements and elevates the visual narrative.

Elements of it are: 

- Composition:Composing original music that aligns with the mood, theme, and pacing of the visual content.

-Synchronization: Timing music segments to coincide perfectly with specific scenes, transitions, and actions within the visual media.

- Emotional Enhancement:Using music to amplify or modify the emotional impact of a scene, thereby guiding the audience’s emotional response.

- Thematic Consistency:Developing recurring musical themes or motifs that help to unify the story and deepen the audience’s connection to characters or concepts.

AI auto scoring for creator gives you the same  level of production as a large studio.
