Between 2007 and 2022, antidepressant prescriptions increased by over 65%, with one in six Americans now on psychiatric medication (CDC, 2022).

Overdiagnosis of ADHD has led to prescriptions tripling from 1997 to 2020, primarily among children and adolescents (JAMA, 2021).
