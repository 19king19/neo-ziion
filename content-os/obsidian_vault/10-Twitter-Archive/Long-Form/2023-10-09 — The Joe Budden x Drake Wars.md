# The Joe Budden x Drake Wars

The Joe Budden x Drake Wars

I haven’t digested the album to give a true opinion on the work. I judge albums based on the artist objective and whether they completed that goal musically and conceptually. Whether I personally like it  or not .

Joe budden made a pivot into media and open the doors for a new industry which is to applauded. 

Drake is a titan in an industry that not many people can navigate. And when you at the 🔝 everyone feels comfortable taking shots. I think you’re allowed to feel however you want and respond however you feel . 

But I don’t think the subject of success in music was fair point . Because we know industry plays games that have nothing to do with talent or good music. 

Having a music career in today age is more about power than skill. #19keys
