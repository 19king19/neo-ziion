# The worst use of AI is to do exactly what everyone else is

The worst use of AI is to do exactly what everyone else is doing—becoming a copy of a copy. 

When you scroll without thought, consume without creating, you stop training the AI and let it train you. 

Social media isn’t for passive consumption; it’s for active creation. 

Otherwise, you’re becoming artificially intelligent: copy, mimic, produce. 

Don’t let the machine mold you into its own image. Be the steward, not the slave. 

Do what humans do. Think.
