# Your ideas can brilliant but if the world hasnt finished reorganizing

Your ideas can brilliant but if the world hasn’t finished reorganizing to receive them at scale you must be careful. 

Wait for synchronization. 

If you scale prematurely you will be diluted misinterpreted or turned into entertainment

Instead control your pace and allow your influence to condense. 

Right now the world is testing your frameworks through individuals

People apply your thinking privately
•in their businesses
•their leadership
•their identity decisions

They do not always credit you publicly
because early adoption happens quietly
