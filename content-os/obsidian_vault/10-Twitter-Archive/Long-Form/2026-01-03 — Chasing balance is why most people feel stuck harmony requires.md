Chasing balance is why most people feel stuck; harmony requires unequal parts moving in the right order, not equal effort in every direction. 

Balance is tension between equal parts, constantly correcting, constantly resisting.

Harmony is unequal parts flowing together, each element in its proper role and rhythm.
