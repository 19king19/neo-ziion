# Fix Yourself or Fix the System

Fix Yourself or Fix the System

Personal struggle is framed as a character flaw rather than a predictable outcome of engineered environments.

As a result, people exhaust themselves correcting symptoms while the underlying structure remains intact.

To understand power today, one must examine not only institutions, but the psychological narratives used to protect them.
