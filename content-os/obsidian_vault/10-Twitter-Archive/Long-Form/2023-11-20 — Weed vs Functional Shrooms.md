# Weed vs Functional Shrooms

Weed vs. Functional Shrooms 

Neurogenesis Support: 

Functional mushrooms, especially Lion's Mane, are revered for their ability to stimulate the production of Nerve Growth Factor (NGF). 

This protein is crucial for the growth, maintenance, and survival of neurons. 

Enhanced neurogenesis means a more robust, resilient neural network, leading to improved memory and cognitive agility.
