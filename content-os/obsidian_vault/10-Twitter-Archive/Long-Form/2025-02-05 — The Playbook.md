# The Playbook

The Playbook:

Infiltration: Identify leaders who can be flattered, funded, or frightened into compliance.

Redirection: Shift movements from structural change (land, resources, revolution) to symbolic wins (voting rights, diversity hires).

Neutralization: Isolate radicals (Malcolm X, Fred Hampton) while promoting “acceptable” leaders (Rustin post-1960s, later MLK’s sanitized legacy).
