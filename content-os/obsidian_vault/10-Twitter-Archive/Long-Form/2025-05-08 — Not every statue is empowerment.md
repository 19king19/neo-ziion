# Not every statue is empowerment

Not every statue is empowerment.

Representation without elevation is just reflection.

And not everything that mirrors us is meant to mold us.

Public art should immortalize what we aspire to not normalize what we’re stuck in.
We need symbols that activate the mind, awaken the spirit, and challenge the culture to rise.

Afro-Futurism. Afro-Cosmism. .
Not this.
