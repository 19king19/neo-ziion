# You dont have a money problem You have a mind problem

You don’t have a money problem. You have a mind problem.

If your thoughts are scattered, your income will be too. If your thinking is reactive, your life will be repetitive. Money is the byproduct of a well-developed mind. It moves toward clarity, discipline, structure, and purpose.

Your value isn’t hidden in your bank account. It’s buried in your undeveloped potential. The better you think, the more you build. The more you build, the more money comes.

Fix the mind. The money will follow.

That’s the key. #Keyism
