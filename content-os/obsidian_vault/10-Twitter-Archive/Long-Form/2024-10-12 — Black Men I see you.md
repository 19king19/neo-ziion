# Black Men I see you

Black Men I see you. 

We’ve are the most targeted & unappreciated group in the world. 

Yet also the most resilient , powerful and innovative , influential , creative and revolutionary men in the world.  

Our strides may go unnoticed , our work may go in rewarded but we know how much courage and brilliance it requires to be us. 

We’re repairing generations with our actions and self development , we materializing what was deprived from us and instilling that into our seeds. 

We’re manifesting our reality through our work while healing and raising ourselves to be better than our fathers. 

We’re investing , educating and leading as 1st generation curse breakers. We are the most admired and copied men in history yet we still get no credit. 

It’s our time and no politics, politician or politician moment will define us or can erase the process we have made against impossible odds. 

I honor all of my brothas who struggling mentally and spiritually to hold stead fast and keep your mind with God. 

Because our time is now to save ourselves.
