# Machine Learning is great but its not exactly Artificial Intelligence

Machine Learning is great but it’s not exactly Artificial Intelligence in the way we think of it.

It’s more aspirational than actual. 

I don’t think it’s earned AI name just yet. 

Especially if we’re comparing it to human cognitive process which are infinitely more complex. 

Artificial means  human made. So it’s not technically wrong. But the connotation of AI is connecting to sentience or consciousness. 

Which in my OPINION it’s not even close.
