# Most people dont realize that the first thing modern society teaches

Most people don't realize that the first thing modern society teaches you is not reading, math, or discipline. It teaches you how to receive information without examining the system that produced it.

You learn how to respond, not how to orient. You learn how to comply, not how to think.

And that one distinction explains why so many intelligent people feel stuck, scattered, and under-realized in a world overflowing with opportunity.
