# AI companies are not just spending billions on building machines

AI companies are not just spending billions on building machines. 

They are spending billions shaping the information environment around those machines.

Our perception of AI is not neutral. It is heavily influenced by marketing budgets, press cycles, venture narratives, and institutional hype designed to justify valuation.

This is why AI feels inevitable, omnipotent, and urgent. Not because it is conscious or complete, but because the story around it is aggressively funded.
