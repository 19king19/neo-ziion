# The Four Steps of Value Leadership

The Four Steps of Value Leadership

Step one is value creation. 

You create ideas, frameworks, platforms, content, education, and pathways that people choose to engage with.

Step two is compensation. 

The community pays because something useful, clarifying, or empowering was delivered.

Step three is reinvestment. 

That compensation is converted back into time, money, travel, education, mentorship, institutions, and opportunities that elevate the same community.

Step four is compounding. 

The people helped then build families, businesses, skills, and confidence, creating more value than they started with. 

Systems grow when value circulates.
