# Every empire has blood on its hands The Moors are no exception

Every empire has blood on its hands. The Moors are no exception.

But history isn’t just in books—it’s in what’s taught, funded, filmed, and forgotten.
Rome gets blockbusters. The Moors get footnotes.

Most never heard of Córdoba, but see Moorish geometry in Masonic temples.
We’re not here to glorify—we’re here to complete the record.
Good, bad, and buried.
