# When a student flunks a class we blame the student

When a student flunks a class, we blame the student.

When an adult flunks a course, we still blame the teacher.

But the truth is once you’re grown, the grade is on your execution, not their explanation.

School had teachers. Life has facilitators.
But mastery? That’s on you.
