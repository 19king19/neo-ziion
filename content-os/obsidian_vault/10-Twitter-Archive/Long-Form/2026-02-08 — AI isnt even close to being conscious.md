# AI isnt even close to being conscious

AI isn’t even close to being conscious. 

It’s very useful machine learning. 

It’s not conscious. It’s not AGI. 

It’s coordinated systems. Mimicking aspect of cognition. 

But Human cognition is not just complex. It is orders of magnitude beyond anything we have ever engineered. Mathematically, biologically, developmentally, we do not fully understand it. 

And if we fail to recognize how greater we are than the tool, then we give reverence to a tool and respect to it, and it influences us, versus we utilize it as an extension of an aspect of our own cognition, not a replication.
