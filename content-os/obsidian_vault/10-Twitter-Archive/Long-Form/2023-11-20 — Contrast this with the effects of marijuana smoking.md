# Contrast this with the effects of marijuana smoking

Contrast this with the effects of marijuana smoking:

Temporary Cognitive Impairment: While marijuana may offer short-term relaxation, it can impair memory, alter judgment, and hinder complex cognitive functions, especially when used excessively.

Potential for Dependence: Regular use of marijuana can lead to dependency, impacting one's ability to function optimally without it.

Lung Health Concerns: Smoking marijuana can harm lung tissues and impair lung function, which indirectly affects brain health due to reduced oxygenation
