# Unpopular Truth Pan-Africanism without power is cosplay

Unpopular Truth: Pan-Africanism without power is cosplay.

 We drape in kente, hashtag #WakandaForever, and scream "Afrika!"—meanwhile, Europe bankrolls our economies, foreign armies guard our leaders, and China strip-mines our resources.

Nations aren’t built on aesthetics, they’re built on systems.

Real Pan-Africanism is a power blueprint, not a costume party:

A continental currency to dismantle the CFA franc & neo-colonial debt traps.
A Black NATO—because no real nation outsources its security.
A Pan-African trade empire that bypasses Western chokeholds.
Land ownership, industrialization, and resource control—not just diaspora selfies in Accra.

 True Pan-Africanism isn’t aesthetic—it’s smashing borders drawn by Europeans and building factories, not just posting dashikis.  

The West loves your symbolic Pan-Africanism. 

It’s the real movement of building your own world that disruptive.
