## Quote Format

Each quote note contains:
- **Quote text** (exact words)
- **Source** (video title, timestamp)
- **Theme tags** (linked to [[04-Themes/]])
- **Quotability score** (1-10, assessed by Claude)
- **Usage suggestions** (social media, book, merch, etc.)

---

## How Quotes Are Captured

1. Whisper transcribes content with timestamps
2. Claude identifies quotable moments (concise, powerful, original)
3. Each quote becomes a standalone note with metadata
4. Auto-linked to themes, frameworks, and source content
