# Not Psychadelic shrooms Im talking functional mushrooms

Not Psychadelic shrooms I’m talking functional mushrooms 🍄 .. 

Psychedelic mushrooms like Psilocybin open doors to altered states of consciousness, offering profound introspection and spiritual experiences. 

Functional mushrooms, on the other hand, are about enhancing everyday brain health – boosting memory, focus, and overall cognitive function without the psychedelic journey. 

Both have their unique roles in  mental exploration. #MushroomWisdom #BrainHealth"
