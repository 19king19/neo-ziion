# Why BET should be black owned

Why BET should be black owned. 

1.Authentic Voice: Black-owned media safeguards the truth of our stories, securing genuine portrayals that encompass the rich tapestry of our experiences.

2.Economic Liberation: Black ownership cultivates self-reliance, circulating wealth within our community and driving progress.

3.Content Sovereignty: Ownership by us guarantees our power to shape the media narrative, reflecting our values and aspirations.

4.Platform for Brilliance: Black-owned media amplifies our creativity, providing a stage for our innovators and visionaries to thrive.
