# The 2nd biggest bank collapse in history this could cause several

The 2nd biggest bank collapse in history this could cause several scenarios to unfold. 

“Silicon Valley Bank collapse is a Big deal”

1 . Systemic risk from a widespread fear and loss of confidence causing other bank to face similar issues. 

2. Contagion effect : This could lead to a cascading effect, causing more banks to collapse and the financial system to spiral out of control

3. Economic slowdown deeper recession :  fewer banks lending money, individuals & businesses will have access to credit, which leads to less investment & economic activity.

4. Market Volatility :  investors panicking & selling off stocks and other assets.
