# The formula for focus is Awareness Attention Concentration Discipline

The formula for focus is Awareness + Attention + Concentration + Discipline. 

Awareness involves being aware of your thoughts, feelings, and environment in order to stay on track. 

Attention requires actively focusing on the task at hand. 

Concentration requires being able to stay focused for extended periods of time. 

And discipline requires being able to stay motivated and self-disciplined in order to complete tasks and reach goals.
