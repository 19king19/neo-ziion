# But heres the problem when the tools go offline you go blank

But here’s the problem: when the tools go offline, you go blank.

AI isn’t the enemy dependence is.
Most people aren’t using AI as an extension of thought; they’re using it instead of thought. 

That’s how you enter cognitive debt. And it’s a debt most won’t be able to pay off because they’ve lost the ability to think critically, deeply, or originally.
